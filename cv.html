Executive Profile
Beth Rudden is Founder & CEO of Bast AI, a former IBM Distinguished Engineer, and a board-level advisor whose work spans trustworthy AI, data lineage, ontology systems, education, and institutional governance.
Her work sits at the intersection of executive decision-making, AI architecture, workforce transformation, and public accountability: the terrain institutions face as AI becomes part of learning, operations, governance, and employer expectations.
