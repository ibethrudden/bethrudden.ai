Beth Rudden

CEO & Co-Founder, Bast AI

Archaeologist of memory systems. Building explainable AI infrastructure where every answer shows its work.

Beth Rudden is the CEO and co-founder of Bast AI, a Denver-based company building infrastructure that lets hospitals, universities, and public institutions deploy AI they can verify, explain, and trust. She holds more than 50 patents and publications, co-authored AI for the Rest of Us, and was recognized as one of the 100 Most Brilliant Leaders in AI Ethics (2023). She holds degrees in Anthropology and Classics.
